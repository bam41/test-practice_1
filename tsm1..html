      <p><span>In this page I will be talking briefly</span>  about <em>DMT</em>. This is a chemical compound known as <strong>Dimethyltryptamine</strong>, otherwise known as the spirit molecule. This is a traditionally shamanistic psychoactive, or hallucinogenic drug, found all over the world but mostly used in past lives. This chemical compound is one of the most powerful in the world, and can be taken in a few different ways:</p>
      <ol>
          <li>Ingested(with MAOI)</li>
          <li>Inhaled when vaporized</li>
          <li>Injected</li>
      </ol> 
   
      <p>This drug is not only powerful, but empowering. People have reported seeing incredible, inter-dimensional beings, as well as having what is called an <em>"ego death."</em> In South America there is a very spiritual place where people can go, if worthy, to stay there for a period of time to heal. While there, they  will have rituals drinking <strong>Ayahuasca</strong> - <em>edible form of DMT</em> - to overcome personal trials from the past, present or future. It has had an extremely high success rate for helping everyone.</p>
